MY METHODS
Data Collection
  • Accessed the City of Chicago Data Portal.
  • Downloaded the dataset related to parks, including attributes like park name, type, and location coordinates.
Data Cleaning and Preparation
  • Filtered out entries with missing or incomplete location or type data.
  • Verified geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) for accuracy, ensuring they map correctly within Chicago boundaries.
  • Categorized parks by type, creating classifications like neighborhood parks, community parks, natural areas, etc. Data Analysis
Data Analysis
  • Counted and classified the types of parks available in each area to see if any specific types are more common in affluent neighborhoods.
Data Visualizations
  • Created a heat map to display park density across Chicago. This map highlights areas with higher and lower concentrations of parks.
  • Designed a bar graph to represent the distribution of different park types, that will eventually be segmented by affluent vs. less affluent neighborhoods for comparison.
